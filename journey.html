Gods and Goddesses

Olympian Gods

Zeus

King of the gods, ruler of Mount Olympus.

Hera

Queen of the gods, goddess of marriage.

Poseidon

God of the sea and earthquakes.

Demeter

Goddess of harvest and fertility.

Athena

Goddess of wisdom and war strategy.

Apollo

God of the sun, music, and prophecy.

Artemis

Goddess of the hunt and the moon.

Ares

God of war and battle lust.

Aphrodite

Goddess of love and beauty.

Hephaestus

God of fire and craftsmanship.

Hermes

Messenger of the gods.

Hestia

Goddess of the hearth and home.

Dionysus

God of wine and theatre.

Chthonic Gods (Underworld)

Hades

God of the Underworld.

Persephone

Queen of the Underworld.

Thanatos

God of Death.

Hecate

Goddess of magic and witchcraft.

Primordial Deities

Gaia

Primordial goddess of Earth.

Uranus

Primordial god of the Sky.

Nyx

Primordial goddess of Night.

Other Deities

Eros

God of love and desire.

Nike

Goddess of victory.

Nemesis

Goddess of balance and retribution.

Pan

God of the wild and shepherds.
